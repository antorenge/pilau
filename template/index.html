About Us

The Computer Society of Kimathi is an award winning society dedicated to the students of Dedan Kimathi University of Technology.

Our Vision

To advance modern technology

Our Mission

To equip club members with technological skills, knowledge and experience.

The Club

The Computer Society of Kimathi (also known as CSK or CSoK) is an academic university club of the Dedan Kimathi University of Technology. The club received its recognition by the University on 12th September 2012, but the origins date back to June 2012 when a group of friends, at the time Students, founded the club hoping to share knowledge on technology through peer to peer teaching and collaborative events such as hackathons.

What we do

EVENTS

We organize hackathons and exhibitions. Hackathons are weekend-sprint development activities where we build amazing software solutions collaboratively. Software Development exhibitions allow our members to exhibit their software projects.

TRAININGS

We facilitate trainings both within and outside the institution. Our local trainings are known as meetups which take place weekly and are fashioned in a peer-to-peer teaching mode. Other form of trainings range from talks to workshops on software development and entrepreneurship.

COMMUNITY OUTREACH

We love our community and feel so much indebted to them. With that we plan to visit at least one childrens' home and take at least one community outreach activity semesterly.

RECENT ACTIVITIES

MEETUPS

Meetups are peer-to-peer teaching sessions which take place within a month of each semester. Some of the topics discussed include programming languages such as PHP, Androi, Ruby, Python and Office Packages.

HACKATHONS

A hackathon is a weekend-sprint development activity where developers, designers, project managers, and technologists come together to solve real world problems through integration and development of new technology within a limited time span of less than 48 hours. See Past Hackathons

TALKS & TRAININGS

We organize valuable talks and trainings from renowned industry experts and experienced persons. Some of our recent talks include the innovation talk from iHub Outreach team. Our trainings include the Microsoft Appstudio Training.

COMMUNITY OUTREACH

Our community outreach programme allows us to perform charitable tasks and social activities. Some include the recent Huruma Childrens Home Visit.
